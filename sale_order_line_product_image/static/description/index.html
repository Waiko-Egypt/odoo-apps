Display Product Images on Quotations, Orders & Invoices

Enhance your business documents by automatically rendering each product's image inside Sale Order, Quotation, and Invoice PDF reports — as well as the sale order line list view inside Odoo. With a single toggle in company settings, every report line shows a clear product photo next to the product name, making documents instantly recognisable to your customers.

Key Features

🖼️

Product Image Column

Adds a dedicated, toggleable image column to the sale order line list inside the form view.

📃

Quotation & Order PDF

Product images appear automatically in the sale quotation / order PDF report table.

🧾

Invoice PDF Report

Product images are also rendered inside customer invoice PDF reports.

⚙️

One-Click Setup

Enable or disable image display globally from Sales → Configuration → Settings or Accounting → Settings.

📐

Custom Image Size

Control the exact width and height (in pixels) of product images in reports to match your brand.

🏢

Multi-Company Ready

Settings are stored per company, supporting multi-company Odoo environments out of the box.

How It Works

Three simple steps to enrich your documents with product images

1

Install the Module

Upload and install from the Apps menu. No configuration wizard required.

2

Enable in Settings

Go to Sales → Configuration → Settings → Product Image in Reports and toggle on "Show Product Image". Optionally adjust the image width and height.

3

Print or Preview

Open any Sale Order or Invoice and click Print. Product images appear automatically in the report table.
