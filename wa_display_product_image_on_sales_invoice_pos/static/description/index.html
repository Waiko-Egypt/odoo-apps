Display Product Images on Quotations, Invoices & Point of Sale

Enhance your business documents by automatically rendering each product's image inside Quotations, Sale Orders, Customer Invoices, and Point of Sale (POS) receipts & screens. With a single toggle in company settings, every report line shows a clear product photo next to the product name — making documents instantly recognisable to your customers.

Key Features

01

Product Image Column

Adds a dedicated, toggleable image column to the sale order line list inside the form view.

02

Quotation & Order PDF

Product images appear automatically in the sale quotation / order PDF report table.

03

Invoice PDF Report

Product images are also rendered inside customer invoice PDF reports.

04

Point of Sale Support

Product images are displayed on POS receipts and product lines for a richer customer experience.

05

One-Click Setup

Enable or disable image display globally from Sales → Configuration → Settings or Accounting → Settings.

06

Custom Image Size

Control the exact width and height (in pixels) of product images in reports to match your brand.

07

Multi-Company Ready

Settings are stored per company, supporting multi-company Odoo environments out of the box.

08

Seamless Integration

Works out-of-the-box with the standard Sales, Invoicing and POS modules — no custom themes required.

How It Works

Three simple steps to enrich your documents with product images

1

Install the Module

Upload and install from the Apps menu on Odoo 13 through 19. No configuration wizard required.

2

Enable in Settings

Go to Sales → Configuration → Settings → Product Image in Reports and toggle on "Show Product Image". Optionally adjust the image width and height.

3

Print, Preview or Sell

Open any Quotation, Invoice or POS session — product images appear automatically in the reports and POS interface.
