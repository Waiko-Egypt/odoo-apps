Key Features

Everything you need to manage warranty across the sales cycle.

1

New Warranty Tab on Product Form

A dedicated Warranty tab is added to the product page. Configure duration and period type per product.

2

Monthly or Yearly Periods

Choose between Months or Years with a single selection — no manual calculations required.

3

Auto-Calculated Start & End Dates

Warranty starts on invoice validation date and ends after the configured duration — computed automatically.

4

Visible in Sale Order Lines

Warranty column shows up on Sale Order lines so your team knows exactly what's covered before the sale.

5

Full Invoice Integration

Invoice lines display warranty duration plus Start / End dates, and print them on the customer invoice.

6

Point of Sale Support

POS orders that generate invoices automatically populate warranty dates on the resulting invoice lines.

7

Clean PDF Reports

Customized Sales & Invoice templates with dedicated warranty column and styled SCSS layout.

8

Zero Configuration

Install and use immediately — no setup required, no wizards, no complex permissions.

How It Works

A simple four-step workflow — from product setup to printed invoice.

1

Configure Product

Open a product and set its warranty duration & period type in the Warranty tab.

2

Create Sale Order

The warranty column shows up automatically on order lines.

3

Validate Invoice

Start & end dates are auto-filled the moment the invoice is posted.

4

Print & Deliver

Print the invoice — warranty details are already on the PDF.
